WELCOME TO THE KINGDOM OF ETHER

Chanakya Neeti:
"जो व्यक्ति अपने ज्ञान को छिपाता है, वह अपने धन को भी खो देता है।"
"A person who hides their knowledge also loses their wealth."
Bhagavad Gita (Chapter 4, Verse 38):
"ज्ञानं परमगुह्यं मे, यद्विज्ञानमुत्तमम्।
"In this world, there is nothing as purifying as knowledge."
